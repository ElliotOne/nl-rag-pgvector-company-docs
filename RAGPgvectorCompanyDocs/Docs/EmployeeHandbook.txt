Introduction:
Welcome to [Company Name]! This handbook provides guidelines to ensure a productive, respectful, and safe workplace. All employees are expected to follow these policies.

Attendance Policy:

Employees must report absences to their manager as soon as possible.

Punctuality is required for all scheduled work hours.

Repeated unexcused absences may result in disciplinary action.

Code of Conduct:

Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect.

Avoid conflicts of interest and maintain professional integrity.

Keep company information confidential.

Paid Time Off (PTO):

Employees accrue PTO according to tenure.

PTO requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

PTO may be used for vacation, illness, or personal reasons.
